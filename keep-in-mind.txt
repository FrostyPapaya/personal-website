How to cannibalize properly:
Pick one feature at a time  
e.g., “I want a sidebar”, “I want a pixel-art cursor”, “I want a grid layout”.

Find 2–3 examples  
Compare how different people solved it.

Extract the smallest possible snippet  
Don’t copy the whole stylesheet — just the relevant 10–20 lines.

Rewrite it in your own naming style  
Change class names, reorganise the HTML.

Add one improvement  
A colour change, a transition, a spacing tweak — anything.
